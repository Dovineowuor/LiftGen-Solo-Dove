# LiftGen Project — Elevator Energy Harvesting & Optimization System

**LiftGen** is a retrofit energy-harvesting and optimization platform designed for elevators.
Its purpose is to **capture wasted mechanical energy from lift motion, store it intelligently, and reuse it to power elevator systems, building loads, and telemetry infrastructure.**

The idea is simple but powerful:
**every elevator already moves enormous mass thousands of times a day.**
LiftGen turns that motion into usable electricity instead of letting it disappear as vibration, friction, and heat.

---

# Core Concept

Elevators constantly generate mechanical energy through:

* Guide rail friction
* Cabin vibration
* Counterweight motion
* Buffer compression
* Structural oscillations

LiftGen installs distributed **energy harvesting nodes** throughout the lift shaft to convert those mechanical effects into electricity.

That electricity is then:

1. **Harvested**
2. **Stored**
3. **Optimized**
4. **Reused**

All without interfering with elevator safety systems.

As the handbook states, the system is intentionally **non-operational and non-safety related**, meaning the elevator functions normally even if LiftGen is completely disconnected. 

---

# System Architecture

LiftGen works as a **distributed micro-energy system inside the elevator infrastructure.**

### 1. Energy Harvesting Nodes

Installed at strategic mechanical points:

• Lift shoe nodes (guide rail friction)
• Cabin floor nodes (vibration)
• Counterweight nodes (vertical motion)
• Buffer nodes (impact compression)

These nodes use:

* **Piezoelectric generators**
* **Micro linear generators**
* **Vibration harvesters**

They convert mechanical movement into low-voltage electrical energy.

---

# Energy Aggregation

All harvested energy flows to the **LiftGen Energy Hub**.

Inside the hub:

• Schottky diode combiners merge input sources
• Supercapacitors absorb fast energy spikes
• Battery storage provides longer-term energy retention

Two storage technologies are used:

**Supercapacitors**

* extremely fast charge/discharge
* ideal for motion spikes

**LiFePO₄ batteries**

* long-duration storage
* safe chemistry
* supports backup power

---

# Energy Reuse

Stored energy can then be redirected to:

### Elevator Systems

* control electronics
* cabin lighting
* ventilation
* auxiliary loads

### Building Systems

* corridor lighting
* building sensors
* BMS systems
* emergency micro-loads

### DC Bus Support

In advanced configurations, the system can supplement the **lift drive DC bus**, helping reduce grid draw during peak loads.

---

# Telemetry & Intelligence Layer

LiftGen is not only about energy—it also becomes a **sensor network for elevator health**.

Every node can report:

* vibration levels
* energy harvested per trip
* component wear patterns
* system health metrics

Telemetry is transmitted through a gateway and visualized via dashboards.

This enables:

• predictive maintenance
• ride quality monitoring
• energy analytics
• operational optimization

---

# Safety Philosophy

Elevator engineering has a sacred rule:

**Never touch the safety chain.**

LiftGen follows that philosophy strictly.

The system:

* does not control the motor
* does not affect braking
* does not interact with door locks
* does not interfere with safety circuits

It is designed to be **electrically isolated** from safety-critical components. 

If LiftGen fails, the elevator continues operating normally.

# The Bigger Vision

LiftGen transforms elevators from **pure energy consumers into micro energy producers.**

In a building with multiple lifts, the system becomes a **small distributed energy network** that:

* harvests motion energy
* stores it locally
* redistributes it intelligently

Over time, elevators become part of the **building’s energy ecosystem**, not just a load on it.
